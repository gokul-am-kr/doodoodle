Meet Your Host

Meet Gokul

Hi, I am Gokul — a passionate doodler and the creator of the Doo Doodle method. I believe doodling is one of the most underrated creative skills in the world. It is simple, joyful, and deeply powerful — for the mind, for focus, and for self-expression.

I created Doo Doodle to make doodling structured, progressive, and genuinely fun for everyone — kids and adults alike. My 6-phase, 20-session method takes complete beginners from their very first line all the way to creating expressive doodle artwork they are proud of.

I cannot wait to share this world with you at the seminar!
