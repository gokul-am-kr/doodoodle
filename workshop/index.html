What to Expect

A workshop unlike any class.

No grades, no pressure, no right or wrong. Just you, a pen, a page — and Gokul guiding every mark.

✏️

Draw from Scratch

Start with simple shapes and build real, expressive characters. No experience needed — if you can draw a circle, you can doodle.

🎨

Colour & Bring to Life

Use markers and colour blocking to make your doodles vibrant. Leave with a finished piece you are proud of.

🧘

Mindful Doodling

Explore zentangle patterns — meditative, repetitive, and deeply calming. A screen-free hour that resets your mind.

👨‍👩‍👧

For Kids & Adults

Separate sessions for kids (5–14) and adults (18+), or family batches where parents and children doodle together.
